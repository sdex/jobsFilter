## jobsFilter - a little helper to diminish junk jobs from the feed 

## Install 
- [FireFox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/jobs-filter/)

## Filters
- the countries
- the title keywords

## Privacy
This extension does not collect any of your private information
